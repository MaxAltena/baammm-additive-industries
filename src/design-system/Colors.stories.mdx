# Colors

Colors give the otherwise bland components a more distinct feel, and at the same time help apply
Additive Industries' look and feel to the product. Several colors are used in the components,
such as the primary, secondary and accent colors to highlight features and create a
distinct look between different items. The status colors are used to consistently give the users an indication
of statuses in the system, making it easily recognizable whenever a machine, or a job, requires immediate attention.
